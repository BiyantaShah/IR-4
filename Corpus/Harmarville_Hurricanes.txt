Harmarville Hurricanes The Harmarville Hurricanes Soccer Club were an amateur soccer club from Harmarville Pennsylvania a suburb of Pittsburgh that twice won the Lamar Hunt US Open Cup known then as the National Challenge Cup in 1952 and 1956 They also played in the Cup Final in 1953 The 1956 Final saw the Hurricanes win 3 2 on aggregate score after a comeback from a two-goal deficit they lost the first leg on the road 1 0 and conceded the first goal in the return home leg 1 Honours edit US Open Cup Champion -- 1952 1956 Runner-up -- 1953 National Amateur Cup Runner-Up -- 1950 1951 edit edit Harmarville Hurricane blew away competition Pittsburgh Post-Gazette August 30 1990 Retrieved May 28 2010 1 - Pittsburgh Post-Gazette date July 13 2003 accessdate May 28 2010
