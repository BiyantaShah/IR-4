Westfort Hurricanes The Westfort Hurricanes were a Canadian Junior ice hockey club from Fort William Ontario The Herks were members of the Thunder Bay Junior A Hockey League and were Abbott Cup finalists once History edit In 1972 the Hurricanes broke away from the Thunder Bay Junior A Hockey League to join the St Paul Minnesota-based Can-Am Junior Hockey League A year later that league became the Midwest Junior Hockey League The Herks stayed on board for one Midwest Junior season before returning to the TBJHL The MWJHL later merged and solidified the United States Hockey League The Buccaneers folded on July 6 1980 when the TBAHA left them with no league to play in Season-by-season standings edit Playoffs edit 1971 Lost Final Westfort Hurricanes defeated Fort William Canadians
